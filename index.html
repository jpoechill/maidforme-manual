Company Mission & Values

MaidForMe is committed to delivering reliable, detail-oriented, and professional home cleaning with a personal touch.

  • Respect clients’ homes
  • Show up on time
  • Clean with care and consistency
  • Communicate clearly and kindly

General Expectations

  • Arrive 5–10 minutes early
  • Wear uniform (t-shirt or apron), clean shoes or shoe covers
  • No phone use during cleaning unless it's an emergency
  • Never disclose any information about clients

Cleaning Protocol by Room

Kitchen

  • Clean countertops & backsplash
  • Wipe appliances
  • Clean inside microwave
  • Empty trash
  • Mop floors

Bathroom

  • Scrub toilet, tub, sink, shower
  • Wipe mirrors
  • Sanitize counters & fixtures
  • Empty trash, mop floors

Bedroom

  • Dust, make bed, wipe mirrors
  • Vacuum or mop floors

Living Areas

  • Dust, fluff pillows, vacuum
  • Clean glass surfaces

Quality Checklist

  • All rooms match client request
  • No supplies or trash left behind
  • Everything looks tidy and smells fresh
  • Lights off and doors locked if needed

Cleaning Products & Supplies

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products unless otherwise requested.

  • Microfiber cloths
  • Vacuum, mop
  • All-purpose cleaner
  • Glass cleaner, disinfectant
  • Gloves, duster

Safety Guidelines

  • Never mix chemicals
  • Use gloves for bathrooms
  • Use step stools safely
  • Report anything unusual

Client Communication

  • Be polite and professional
  • Ask for clarification when unsure
  • Let the client know when you're done
  • Direct all complaints or praise to management

Emergency Procedures

  • Call 911 for emergencies
  • Report all incidents to supervisor
  • Text or call dispatch for minor issues

Reporting & Time Tracking

  • Clock in/out using MaidForMe app
  • Submit before/after photos if needed
  • Fill out cleaning report checklist
